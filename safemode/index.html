About Amser SafeMode ×

SafeMode is a limited-use version of Barcaly Amser and designed to be a sort of Amser Lite. All 3rd-party and non-essential apps have been removed from this version to make the experience and load time better and faster. Barcaly Amser SafeMode is designed to only be a last resort if your copy of Amser has experienced a fatal bug. Entering SafeMode may have been an accident, as you can quickly change to it with the CTRL+S shortcut. To return to the normal version of Barcaly Amser, use the CTRL+G shortcut.
